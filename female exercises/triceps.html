Female Triceps Exercises

Triceps Dip

The triceps dip is an effective bodyweight exercise for targeting the triceps. It can be performed on parallel bars or a sturdy chair or bench. The exercise involves lowering the body by bending the elbows and then pushing back up to the starting position using the triceps. It can be modified to make it easier or harder based on fitness level.

Close Grip Bench Press

The close grip bench press is a variation of the traditional bench press that places more emphasis on the triceps. It is performed using a barbell with a narrower grip than the regular bench press, typically shoulder-width or closer. The exercise involves lowering the barbell to the chest and then pressing it back up using the triceps. It is commonly used as a compound exercise for building upper body strength.

Triceps Pushdown

The triceps pushdown is an isolation exercise that targets the triceps using a cable machine. It is performed standing with a straight bar or a rope attachment, and involves pushing the cable down towards the thighs while keeping the elbows stationary. It provides a good stretch and contraction of the triceps muscles, and is commonly used as a finishing exercise for triceps workouts.
