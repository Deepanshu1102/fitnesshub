Female Legs Exercises

Squats

Squats are a compound exercise that targets the lower body, including the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and calves. They are performed by bending at the knees and hips, keeping the back straight, and lowering the body towards the ground. Squats are considered one of the most effective exercises for building leg strength, improving lower body mobility, and enhancing overall lower body power and athleticism.

Leg Press

Leg Press is a machine-based exercise that targets the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and calves. It is performed by pushing a weighted sled with the feet while seated on a machine. Leg Press is an effective exercise for building leg strength, particularly for individuals who may have limitations or difficulties with performing free weight exercises like squats.

Deadlifts

Deadlifts are a compound exercise that targets the entire posterior chain, including the lower back, glutes, hamstrings, and calves. They are performed by lifting a loaded barbell from the ground while maintaining a neutral spine and standing up with the weight. Deadlifts are considered one of the best exercises for building overall strength, power, and muscle mass in the legs and back.
