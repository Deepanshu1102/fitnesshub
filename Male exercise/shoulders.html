Male Shoulders Exercises

Military Press

The military press, also known as the overhead press, is a compound exercise that targets theshoulders. It can be performed with a barbell or dumbbells, and involves pressing the weight overhead from shoulder height, while keeping the core engaged and maintaining proper form. It is a popular exercise for building strength and size in the shoulder muscles.

Dumbbell Lateral Raise

The dumbbell lateral raise is an isolation exercise that targets the lateral deltoids, which are the outer part of the shoulder muscles. It is performed standing or seated, with dumbbells in hand, and involves raising the arms to the sides to shoulder level, while keeping the elbows slightly bent. It is commonly used to isolate and strengthen the side delts for a well-rounded shoulder workout.

Front Raise

The front raise is an isolation exercise that targets the anterior deltoids, which are the front part of the shoulder muscles. It is performed standing or seated, with dumbbells in hand, and involves raising the arms to the front to shoulder level, while keeping the elbows slightly bent. It is commonly used to isolate and strengthen the front delts for a balanced shoulder workout.
