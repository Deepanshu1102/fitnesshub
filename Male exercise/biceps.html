Male Biceps Exercises

Barbell Bicep Curl

The barbell bicep curl is a classic exercise for targeting the biceps. It is typically performed using a barbell and involves curling the weight towards the shoulders while keeping the elbows stationary. It can be done standing or seated, and is often performed with variations such as using an EZ bar or a straight barbell.

Hammer Curl

The hammer curl is a variation of the bicep curl that targets not only the biceps, but also the brachialis muscle which is located underneath the biceps. It is performed using dumbbells with a neutral grip (palms facing each other), and involves curling the weights towards the shoulders while keeping the elbows stationary. It can be done standing or seated.

Concentration Curl

The concentration curl is a bicep exercise that is performed sitting down with a dumbbell in one hand, and the arm resting against the inner thigh. The exercise involves curling the weight towards the shoulder while keeping the elbow stationary, and is known for isolating the biceps and providing a deep contraction. It is commonly used as a finishing exercise for bicep workouts.
